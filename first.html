CENTER FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH(CAIR)

CENTER FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH(CAIR)

Title:Secured Mobile App Voting System for Elderly and Non-Participating People

The percentage of voting is coming down every Indian election due to various reasons. Older adults are not coming to the booth for voting. Officers working in the election duty and people staying in other state are also not voting sometimes. It has decreased the voting percentage in every election. On the other hand, people raise questions about the security of the online voting system. This project proposes AADHAR based solutions with deep learning algorithms based solution to solve these problems. It also offers to use of blockchain technology to ensure the highly secured transactions of the system. The main AADHAR based credentials verification involve the verification of face, thump and iris metrics. The design has the OTP (One time Password) System to ensure secured access. Thus, this project will help to increase voter’s participation at a very high level.

CENTER FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH(CAIR)

Title: Design and Development of a System for Searching Drugs in the Pharmacy

Drug or Medicine Identification is one of the most challenging tasks for Chemists working in the pharmacy. Especially, locating the drug among the medicines is challenging: real-time object detection and tracking system using RFID (Radio-frequency identification) to solve this problem. Our approach uses heterogeneous IoT devices, barcode detection, and an Android application that has a user-friendly interface. Barcode detection into two operations of positioning and data decoding. All the medicine in the drug store will come with an RFID. According to the customer’s requirement, the product’s barcode will be using an android application. The database has the product’s name, cost of the product, Number of quantities available in the android application. The LED blink indicates the location of the product. In this system, everything with wireless communication. This project aims to decrease the aggregate holding uptime and work of the chemists in a drugstore.
